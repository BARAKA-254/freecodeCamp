Cyber Security awareness

We live in a digital era where most attacks happen digitally. In this era, data is one of the most important assets that we posses. This leads to a need for data protection.
This is why we organize events such as this in order to equip people with tricks used by cyber criminals to help them secure themselves against such attacks.
